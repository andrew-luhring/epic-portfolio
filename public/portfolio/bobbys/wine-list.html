Wine Program

Co-Owner and Wine Director Bobby Arifi has traveled the world to bring the most innovative grouping of wines from around the world. The extensive global list features over 240 labels. While we offer over 28 highly unique labels by the glass, the central focus of the program is the high-tech cruvinet system, which perfectly temperature controls 15 featured wines hand-selected by Bobby.
