Our Solutions

Danaco Solutions, LLC is a leading produce and fresh foods supply chain management company delivering industry expertise to its customers in Procurement, Supply Chain Visibility and Food Safety & Quality. Danaco works as an integral extension of its customers' internal teams by providing customized solutions that produce measurable cost savings, reduced food safety risk, and hands-on quality supply management.

Procurement and Spend Management

Danaco's procurement solutions provide cost savings and visibility to price and usage through unique sourcing strategies and contract negotiations, supply management and customized purchasing programs. Danaco offers Conscious Sourcing programs (i.e. local, organic, sustainable, etc.) as part of the procurement solutions offerings.

Visible Cost Savings

Danaco manages pricing and contract negotiations to provide visible cost savings to its customers' bottom line. This is executed through Danaco's consolidated buying power and industry-leading team of dedicated procurement professional. Danaco's commitment to complete pricing and supply transparency builds trust and stability for continual growth and business success.

Fixed and Market-Based Pricing Programs

Danaco offers both fixed and market-based pricing programs to satisfy specific and unique needs of each customer.

Managed Raw Material Supply

Danaco's procurement team provides raw material sourcing and management. Individual customers' needs are looked at separately to attain the finest quality products at the best possible price. Danaco only sources raw material from qualified, approved growers.

Customized Sourcing Programs

Danaco's purchasing team offers a customized approach when developing purchasing programs to meet each customers' needs. Each purchasing model is unique and is developed to maximize savings, enhance product quality, and deliver the right product at an optimal price.

Conscious Sourcing Solutions — Local, Organic, Sustainable, etc.

Danaco has unique expertise and programs set up with local, sustainable, socially accountable, and organic suppliers to meet the growing demand of customers wanting to "do the right thing". Danaco recognizes the marketing impact and social benefit of these sourcing programs. Danaco's management team has extensive experience defining, developing and implementing such programs on a national scale ensuring supply and quality are met year-round.

Order Management

As part of a comprehensive procurement offering, Danaco's Order Management (OM) services offer standardized and centralized order fulfillment at any point in the supply chain. The benefits of Danaco's Order Management include pricing, contract, and product compliance which inclides:

  • Substitution management
  • Credit and claims management
  • Supplier performance monitoring
  • Usage reporting
  • Budgetary and spend analytics

Quality Assurance and Oversight

Danaco Solutions provides product quality evaluations, blinded supplier cuttings, product-quality and training summits for its suppliers and customers. These tools and services provide Danaco's customers a realistic and timely view of what's happening with their product supply, allowing them to make more informed decisions and ensure product quality throughout their supply chain.

Product Evaluations

Danaco has dedicated experienced resources in product sensory. Through the use of Danaco's SRM evaluation tool, QA personnel can quickly input product evaluations on a hand-held, web-based device. Supporting supplier performance management, QA supervisors and staff are set-up to be instantly notified of failed product evaluations and reporting can be produced at the click of a button.

Product Cuttings

Danaco's Food Safety Team conducts supplier cuttings (product sampling) from various distribution points. This gives Danaco's customers insight into what is happening with their product at each touch point throughout their supply chain.

Product Quality Summits and Training

Danaco offers product quality summits that are customized to customer needs. The summit can include suppliers and be set up to review one product or the entire product line. Topics may include QA training and expectations, quality evaluations, transitional product challenges and supply expectations. Training is customized based on the needs of each customer.

Food Safety Validation and Risk Mitigation

Danaco's Food Safety Solutions allow its customers to transform their standard food safety program into a leading-edge food safety service. Danaco's Food Safety solutions include supplier validation and approval, technical food safety guidance, and trace-back management and support.

Supplier Validation and Approval

Danaco's Food Safety Team utilizes the Danaco Supplier Relationship Manager (SRM) to track, schedule and update third party audits to assure the highest standards for its customers. In addition to capturing third party audits, Danaco conducts on-site supplier assessments at all touch-points to further verify each supplier is meeting targeted high food safety standards.

Technical Food Safety Guidance

The knowledge and expertise of Danaco's Food Safety Team make Danaco Solutions a valuable asset and resource for its customers. The team guides and advises its customers through Industry Food Safety requirements that include Supplier Approval, Microbial Testing, Traceability, Recalls, and general Supply Chain Management.

Trace Back Management & Support:

Quick trace-back reporting in the event of a product recall was a key driver in the development of Danaco's Supplier Relationship Management database. The SRM database maps your complex supply chain and quickly captures the entire supply flow of a single product from the grower to the supply end point. Supply chain touch-points and comprehensive reports can be customized to meet individual customer needs.

Food Safety Trainings

Danaco's Food Safety Team works with each customer to meet all of their food safety training needs. These services may include food safety training for internal staff members, produce suppliers, and local or large-scale growers.

Supply Chain Visibility

Today more than ever, you need complete visibility of your entire supply chain, from pricing compliance to specific industry and regulatory requirements necessary by your suppliers. Danaco has developed two systems that work in Harmony with one another to provide you with state-of-the-art intelligence and management reports as it relates to your entire supply chain. Danaco's Supplier Resource Management (SRM) system can capture and manage all relevant pieces of the supply chain including, but not limited to pricing components, contracts, supplier facilities, business contacts, 3rd party audits, on-site assessments and product/supply chain reporting from farm to store.

SRM — Danaco's Supplier Relationship Manager:

Danaco's Supplier Relationship Management System (SRM) is a cloud-based management tool designed to facilitate Danaco's procurement and food safety services providing quick access and management of each customer's supply chain. Danaco's SRM serves as a single reference for all supplier and supply chain information. Key features include:

  • Supply Chain Relationships and Visualization from Source Ingredient to Store
  • Supplier Compliance and Approval Process Management
  • Facility Assessment Capture.
  • Relationship Information Management (e.g. contacts, facilities, addresses, products, etc.)
  • Document Management- contracts, COIs, audits, assessments, testing, etc.
  • Activity Capture and Tracking
  • Product Evaluation Capture
  • Customizable Reporting and Analytics

DAS — Danaco Analytics System

DAS is a proprietary application developed to provide business intelligence and supply chain performance from source to end user. DAS is the tool that allows Danaco to provide transaction level analytics of price compliance, spend management, volume usage, credit management, and other sourcing (e.g. country of origin, organic usage, local usage, etc.) including performance measures.
