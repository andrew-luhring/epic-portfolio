    Welcome to Bobby's Deerfield

    Bobby’s is reminiscent of the iconic, nostalgic neighborhood restaurant of yesteryear.

    We are bringing graciousness back to dining out, in a simple way, by turning out the best product and best service.

    That said, we’ve modernized the old school model. We’re straightforward in our cuisine yet inventive. Its food you can understand but be inspired by. This is our take on a new American bistro but you are certain to see influences from all over the world. That means high-end ingredients, familiar but fresh preparations. And, a wine and craft cocktail list that features an unexpectedly sophisticated list of boutique labels and creative concoctions.

    Our philosophy is easy, provide a quality, consistently-good product that is accessible to all.

    There’s no pretense, no attitude, just genuine friendliness and a desire to please.

    We’re small scale with big expectations and we are all about making certain our customers have a great experience and are comfortable in our space.
